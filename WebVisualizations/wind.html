Latitude vs. Wind Speed

This page explores the relationship between latitude and humidity. Data were pulled from the OpenWeatherMap API for 589 cities on 20 June 2021. Matplotlib was used for the data visualization.

There does not appear to be a relatipnship between latitude (i.e., distance from the equator) and wind speed. In general, most locations seem to have relatively low wind speed (i.e., < 10 mph). As with other figures, the predominance of datapoints in the Northern Hemisphere is simply due to most of the Earth's landmass being in the Northern Hemisphere.
