Latitude vs. Humidity

This page explores the relationship between latitude and humidity. Data were pulled from the OpenWeatherMap API for 589 cities on 20 June 2021. Matplotlib was used for the data visualization.

While there are discernable clusters in the dataset, there does not appear to be a clear relationship between distance from the equator and humidity in this sample. If one had to draw conclusions, it appears that the lowest levels of humidity fell within a range of -20 to +40 latitude. But there are also many locations within that range with high levels of humidity. As with other figures, there are more datapoints for the Northern Hemisphere than the Southern Hemisphere because most of the Earth's landmass is in the Northern Hemisphere.
